QUESTIONS
What sports do you cover?
We cover all major sports — NBA, NFL, MLB, NHL, soccer, and more. If it's a sports card, CollectIQ can analyze it.

Our AI analyzes real sold data, player stats, market trends, and historical patterns. All projections include confidence levels and probability estimates. This is for informational purposes — not financial advice.
