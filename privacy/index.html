PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: March 22, 2026

ParlAI ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and website at tryparlai.com (collectively, the "Service").

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and an encrypted password. We do not store your password in plain text.

Usage Data: We track how many parlays you build per billing period to enforce plan limits. This includes a count of builds — not the content of your parlays.

Payment Information: Payments are processed by Stripe. We never see or store your full credit card number. Stripe handles all payment data under their own privacy policy.

Images You Upload: If you upload screenshots for analysis, images are sent to our AI provider (Anthropic) for processing and are not stored on our servers after the analysis is complete.

Analytics: We use Microsoft Clarity to understand how users interact with our website. This may collect anonymized interaction data such as clicks and scroll behavior. No personal data is shared with advertisers.

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • To provide and maintain the Service
  • To process your subscription payments via Stripe
  • To enforce usage limits based on your plan
  • To generate AI-powered parlay analysis using Anthropic's API
  • To improve the Service through anonymized analytics
  • To communicate with you about your account or changes to the Service

DATA SHARING

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Supabase — authentication and database hosting
  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Anthropic — AI analysis (images and text prompts are sent for processing)
  • Vercel — application hosting
  • Microsoft Clarity — anonymized website analytics

DATA RETENTION

Account data is retained as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data will be removed within 30 days. Anonymized analytics data may be retained indefinitely.

DATA SECURITY

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure authentication tokens, and trusted third-party providers. However, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure.

YOUR RIGHTS

You may:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of analytics by using browser privacy tools

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

ParlAI is intended for users aged 21 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 21. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

CONTACT US

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at tryparlai@gmail.com.
